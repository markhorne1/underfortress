## 🎮 Game Features

### Character System
- SKILL: 9-14 (combat ability)
- STAMINA: 14-26 (health)
- LUCK: 7-13 (fortune)
- Gold & Provisions tracking

### Combat System
- Turn-based with dice rolls (2d6)
- Attack Strength = SKILL + 2d6
- Melee and ranged options
- Flee/retreat mechanics
- 8+ unique enemies
- Boss battles

### Exploration
- 11x11 fog-of-war map
- Multiple story paths
- Secret passages
- Hidden treasures
- Puzzles and riddles

### Inventory
- 15+ items
- Weapons (damage bonus)
- Armor (defense)
- Consumable potions
- Quest items (keys)
- Magical treasures

### Narrative
- 40+ unique pages
- Multiple endings
- Skill/luck tests
- Flag-based progression
- Branching storylines
